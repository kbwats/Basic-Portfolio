About Me

Kristen Watson is a journalist and photographer in the North Texas region and served as editor and photographer for Alexis Bortell's book, Let's Talk About Medical Cannabis. Kristen began a photojournalism project that followed Alexis Bortell called Green Migration in January of 2015, two months before the Bortells decided to move to Colorado. Since, she has followed their story and has expanded the project to include another journalist, Hannah Ridings, to create The Illegal Hope Project, a photojournalism project that aims to document medical marijuana refugee's stories as well as stories of patients stuck in non-medical states. If you or someone you know would like to be a part of this project, please contact Kristen at: illegalhopeproject@gmail.com Kristen is a recent graduate of the University of North Texas' Mayborn School of Journalism and her work has been published by the Dallas Morning News, USA Today, Mother Jones, Mashable, WFAA, CBS DFW, Denton Record-Chronicle, The Dentonite, NeighborsGO, Texas Woman's University and the North Texas Daily. Follow her work on Instagram at @kristenwatsonn or on Twitter at @kbwats.
